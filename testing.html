Atari 2600

1977

was equipped with a joystick and several buttons that allowed players to control characters in the game.

Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)

1985

became the most popular game console at the time with over 60 million units sold. The NES was equipped with a more advanced controller and more color in its graphics.

Sega Genesis

1988

became the most popular game console in the 1990s. The console was equipped with more controller buttons, as well as more advanced graphics than the NES.

Sony PlayStation

1994

became the first game console to use CD-ROM media as a source of games. The console became very popular with over 100 million units sold worldwide

Nintendo 64

1996

became the first game console to use 3D graphics. The Nintendo 64 was also equipped with an innovative controller with a joystick that could be moved in various directions.

Sony PlayStation 2

2000

became the best-selling game console of all time with over 150 million units sold. The PlayStation 2 was equipped with a DVD player that could be used as a movie player.

Xbox 360

2005

became the first game console that could be connected to the internet network and allowed players to play games online. The console was also equipped with multimedia features such as music and video players.

PlayStation 4

2013

became the most popular game console today with over 100 million units sold worldwide. The PlayStation 4 was equipped with features such as a Blu-ray player, media streaming, and motion controllers.

Xbox Series X/S

2020

became the latest game console launched by Microsoft. This console has very high hardware specifications, and allows players to play games with 4K graphics and 120 fps.
